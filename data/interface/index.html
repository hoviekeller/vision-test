How to perform the test

A. Press on the play button at the top-left corner of the interface.

B. Stay 5 feet or 1.6 meters away from the screen (or monitor) and hold one of your eyes.

C. Read the direction of the - E - character at each level (top, bottom, left, or right).

D. Complete all the ten levels for both eyes or stop at the level where you can't read the E letter anymore.

E. Please record your score and visit addon's support page to get more info about your score and its definition.
